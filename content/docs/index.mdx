## Introduction

Welcome to the GDG (Google Developer Groups) documentation. This is your starting point for learning about our community and events.

## Getting Started

Here you'll find comprehensive guides and documentation to help you get involved with GDG.

### What is GDG?

Google Developer Groups (GDG) are community groups for college and university students interested in Google developer technologies.

### Key Features

- **Community Events**: Join our regular meetups and workshops
- **Learning Resources**: Access to tutorials and learning materials
- **Networking**: Connect with fellow developers
- **Hands-on Experience**: Participate in hackathons and coding challenges
